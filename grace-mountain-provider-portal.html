How do you want to be included in proposals?
This choice directly affects how quickly you can get a new client.
⚡ Speed matters more than you might think.

Case managers receive proposals from multiple agencies at once. The agencies that respond fastest — with a confirmed provider and a scheduled meet and greet — almost always win the placement. Every extra step between a referral and a booked meeting costs you time and opportunity.
📅 One thing is true no matter what you choose:

When a referral comes in that you are part of, you need to accept the meet and greet calendar invite as fast as possible — ideally within the hour. A confirmed time on the calendar is what turns a proposal into a placement.
✅
Yes — pre-authorize me
Best chance of getting clients. If a referral matches your profile, Grace Mountain includes you in the proposal immediately — no waiting on a text first. Your job is to accept the calendar invite fast when it arrives.
💬
Case by case — text me first for higher support needs
Slows things down for complex clients. Pre-authorized for SIS Levels 1–3, but Grace Mountain will text you first for Level 4 and above. The back-and-forth adds time — which can mean losing the placement to a faster agency. Still need to accept calendar invites quickly.
📱
Always text me first
Slowest option — limits your placements. Grace Mountain texts you before every proposal. If you do not respond quickly, we may miss the window entirely. Calendar accepts are still urgent when you do get included.

ADA Accessible

A home is generally ADA accessible if it has:

  • No-step entry into the home
  • Doorways at least 32 inches wide
  • At least one accessible bathroom with grab bars
  • Light switches and outlets at reachable heights
  • Lever-style door handles

Not sure?

  • Select Partially if your home meets some criteria
  • Select Not sure and we will help assess together

Wheelchair Accessible

For full wheelchair accessibility:

  • No-step or ramped entry
  • Doorways at least 36 inches wide
  • Bedroom accessible without stairs
  • Bathroom with 60-inch turning radius
  • Roll-in shower or transfer space

Common questions:

  • Fully means the client could navigate independently in a wheelchair
  • Partially means some areas are accessible but not all
  • A portable ramp can sometimes qualify a home — let us know
